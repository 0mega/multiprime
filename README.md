# Multiprime

This gem allows to print multiplication table for first N prime or fibonacci numbers

## Installation

* download the gem
* unpack the gem
* go to gem root dir
* run `rake install`

## Usage

    $ multiprime --alg prime|fib --count N

## Sample output

    $ multiprime --alg prime --count 4

Output:

|   | 2  | 3  | 5  | 7  |
|---|----|----|----|----|
| 2 | 4  | 6  | 10 | 14 |
| 3 | 6  | 9  | 15 | 21 |
| 5 | 10 | 15 | 25 | 35 |
| 7 | 14 | 21 | 35 | 49 |
